What happens if I cancel during the free trial?

If you cancel during the 7-day free trial, you won't be charged. Your account will revert to the free version at the end of the trial period.

Can I change my package plan after subscribing?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your package plan at any time through your account settings.

How does the billing cycle work?

You can choose between monthly or annual billing. Monthly billing charges you every month, while annual billing charges you once a year with a discount.

Will I lose my data if I cancel my subscription?

No, your data will be saved. However, you will lose access to Pro features and your account will be limited to free features.

You can reach out to our customer support team via the "Contact Us" section on our website for any assistance.
